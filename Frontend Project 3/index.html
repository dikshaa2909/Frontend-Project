Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our bootcamp

Do I need prior coding experience?

No prior coding experience is required for our beginner-friendly courses. We start with the fundamentals and gradually build up to more advanced concepts. For specialized courses like Data Science, some basic programming knowledge is helpful but not mandatory.

How much time should I commit each week?

For part-time programs, you should expect to commit 20-25 hours per week, including classes and independent work. Full-time programs require 40+ hours per week, similar to a full-time job. The more time you can dedicate to practice and projects, the better your results will be.

How does the job guarantee work?

Our job guarantee ensures that if you don't secure a job within 6 months of graduation, you'll receive a full refund of your tuition. To qualify, you must complete all coursework, participate in career services activities, and actively apply for relevant positions. Full details are provided in our enrollment agreement.

What kind of support will I receive?

You'll receive comprehensive support throughout your journey, including live instruction, 1-on-1 mentorship sessions, code reviews, career coaching, and access to our student community. Our instructors and mentors are available via chat for quick questions, and you'll have regular check-ins to ensure you're on track.

Are there any financing options available?

Yes, we offer several financing options, including monthly payment plans, income share agreements (ISAs), and partnerships with lending providers. We also offer scholarships for underrepresented groups in tech. Our admissions team can help you find the best option for your situation.

What happens if I fall behind?

We understand that life happens, and sometimes you might need extra support. If you fall behind, your mentor will work with you to create a catch-up plan. We also offer additional tutoring sessions and the option to pause your enrollment and join a later cohort if necessary.
