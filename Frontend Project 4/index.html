Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FitPro AI

How does the AI personalization work?

FitPro AI uses machine learning algorithms to analyze your fitness level, goals, preferences, and progress data. It then creates personalized workout and nutrition plans that adapt in real-time as you progress. The more you use the app, the smarter and more personalized it becomes.

Do I need any special equipment?

Not at all! FitPro AI creates workout plans based on the equipment you have available, including bodyweight-only options. You can update your equipment availability at any time, and the app will adjust your workouts accordingly.

Can I use FitPro AI with my fitness tracker or smartwatch?

Yes! FitPro AI integrates with most popular fitness trackers and smartwatches, including Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin, and more. This allows for more accurate tracking and personalization based on your heart rate, sleep, and activity data.

How often are new workouts and features added?

We add new workouts, recipes, and features every month. Our team of fitness experts and nutritionists is constantly creating new content, and our developers regularly release app updates with new functionality based on user feedback.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. If you cancel within the first 30 days, you'll receive a full refund under our money-back guarantee.

Is my personal data secure?

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal information, and we never sell your data to third parties. You can review our full privacy policy in the app or on our website.
